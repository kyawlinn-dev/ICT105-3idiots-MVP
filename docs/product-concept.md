# Lab 05 - Product Concept

## Project Title
Student Apartment Finder (near Rangsit University)

## Target User
Students searching for off-campus rooms near Rangsit University — especially new and international students — plus apartment owners who list rooms directly and senior students seeking roommates.

## Problem
From Lab 03 evidence, students spend about 2.9 weeks searching scattered sources (116 of 120 used 3+ sources, mainly Facebook groups), with unclear price and distance, and 71 of 120 also struggled to find a roommate.

## Proposed System
A web app where owners post rooms directly (no agents), students search and filter by budget, distance, and room type, view full details with status, contact owners directly, and use a roommate board — with an admin dashboard for oversight.

## Value Proposition
One trusted place to find nearby student rooms with clear price and distance, contact owners directly, and find a roommate — replacing a slow, scattered search.

## MVP Scope (in)
Homepage, browse/search/filter listings, listing detail with status, owner add-listing form, direct owner contact, roommate board, admin management, and an admin dashboard.

## Out of Scope (for now)
Online payment, rental contracts, AI roommate matching, mobile app, and real-time chat (contact details are enough for the MVP).

## How This Guides the Prototype
Every screen maps to a requirement (FR-01 to FR-16), a user story (US-01 to US-16), and an MVP feature — see feature-requirement-mapping.md.
