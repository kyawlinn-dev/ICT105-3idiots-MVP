# Product Concept

## 1. Project Title
Student Apartment Finder (near Rangsit University)

## 2. Target User
Primary users: students searching for off-campus rooms near Rangsit University, especially new and international students. Secondary users: apartment owners who list rooms directly, and senior students seeking roommates.

## 3. Problem Statement
From Lab 03 evidence, students spend about 2.9 weeks searching scattered sources (116 of 120 used 3+ sources, mainly Facebook groups), with unclear price and distance, and 71 of 120 also struggled to find a roommate.

## 4. Proposed IT Product
A web-based apartment finder where owners post rooms directly (no agents), students search and filter by budget, distance, and room type, view full details with status, contact owners directly, and use a roommate board — with an admin dashboard for oversight.

## 5. Core Value Proposition
One trusted place to find nearby student rooms with clear price and distance, contact owners directly, and find a roommate — replacing a slow, scattered search.

## 6. MVP Scope
Homepage, browse/search/filter listings, listing detail with status, owner add-listing form, direct owner contact, roommate board, admin management, and an admin dashboard.

## 7. Out of Scope for Current Semester
- Online payment or deposits
- Rental contract handling
- AI roommate matching
- Native mobile app
- Real-time chat (contact details are enough for the MVP)
