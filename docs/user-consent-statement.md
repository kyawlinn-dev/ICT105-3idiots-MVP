# User Consent Statement - Prototype Testing

## Project

Student Apartment Finder Platform - ICT105 class prototype

## Invitation and Purpose

You are invited to test a class prototype that helps university students search for apartments near campus and review related features such as filters, apartment details, saved listings, roommate posts, and inquiry messages. The purpose of this test is to understand whether the prototype is clear, useful, and easy to use and to identify improvements for the team project.

## Voluntary Participation

Participation is voluntary. You may skip any question or stop testing at any time without penalty. Choosing not to participate will not affect your course, grades, services, or relationship with the project team.

## Data Collected During Testing

The team may record:

- an anonymous participant code such as T01;
- whether assigned tasks were completed;
- time or difficulty observations;
- usability or usefulness ratings;
- comments about the interface and workflow; and
- technical problems encountered during testing.

The team does not need your student ID, passport number, national ID, banking information, account password, OTP code, identity-document image, health information, or other sensitive personal information.

## How the Data Will Be Used

Testing information will be used only for ICT105 class analysis, prototype improvement, project documentation, and final reporting. Results should be summarized or anonymized before they are placed in the public GitHub repository or shown in class evidence.

## Safe Test Data

Please use the sample accounts and sample apartment information supplied by the team. Do not enter real passwords, payment details, government identifiers, private addresses, confidential messages, or photographs containing people or identity documents.

## Possible Risks

The test is low risk, but an open feedback comment or screenshot could accidentally include personal information. The team will review evidence and remove names, contact details, and sensitive content before public upload.

## Questions or Withdrawal

You may ask the project team questions before or during testing. You may request removal of your identifiable testing notes before the results have been anonymized and combined into the class analysis.
