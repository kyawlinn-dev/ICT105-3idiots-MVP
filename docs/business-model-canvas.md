# Business Model Canvas

## Project Title
Student Apartment Finder (near Rangsit University)

## 1. Customer Segments
Students searching for rooms near Rangsit University (especially new and international students); apartment owners who want to rent directly to students; and senior students looking for roommates to share rent.

## 2. Value Proposition
One place to find student rooms near campus with clear price and distance, contact owners directly (no agents), and find a roommate — replacing the ~2.9-week search across scattered Facebook groups found in Lab 03.

## 3. Channels
The web app itself; university and LINE student groups; QR codes and posters on campus notice boards; and word of mouth among students.

## 4. Customer Relationships
Self-service search and listing; direct student-owner contact; admin verification of listings; availability status updates; and a simple roommate board.

## 5. Revenue Streams / Sustainability Logic
Free for students. Sustained through optional owner listing promotions, small featured-listing fees, or university/partner support. For the MVP, the value is operational — saving students time and connecting owners to students — rather than direct revenue.

## 6. Key Resources
The web application, the listings database, the admin role, sample apartment data, and the team's development skills (React/Vite).

## 7. Key Activities
Collecting and verifying listings, running search/filter, showing details and status, managing the roommate board, and maintaining the admin dashboard.

## 8. Key Partners
Apartment owners near campus, the university student council/housing office, student communities, and later local condos and agents who post directly.

## 9. Cost Structure
Hosting and domain, development and maintenance time, admin moderation effort, and optionally the Google Maps API.

## Final Note
This business model connects directly to our MVP features: the listing, search, detail, and roommate features deliver the value proposition, while the admin and dashboard features support platform quality and sustainability. Every block is grounded in Lab 03 evidence (116 of 120 students used 3+ scattered sources; 71 of 120 sought a roommate).
