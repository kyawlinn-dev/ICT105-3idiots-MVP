# Lab 06 - Business Model Canvas

## Project: Student Apartment Finder (near Rangsit University)

| Block | Details for Our Case |
|---|---|
| **Customer Segments** | (1) Students searching for rooms near Rangsit University, especially new and international students; (2) apartment owners who want to rent directly to students; (3) senior students looking for roommates to share rent. |
| **Value Proposition** | One place to find student rooms near campus with clear price and distance, contact owners directly (no agents), and find a roommate — replacing a ~2.9-week search across scattered Facebook groups. |
| **Channels** | The web app itself; university student groups and LINE groups; QR codes/posters on campus notice boards; word of mouth among students. |
| **Customer Relationships** | Self-service search and listing; direct student-owner contact; admin verification of listings; status updates (available/taken); a simple roommate board. |
| **Revenue / Sustainability** | Free for students. Sustained through optional owner listing promotions, small featured-listing fees, or university/partner support. For the MVP the focus is validated usage value, not direct revenue. |
| **Key Resources** | The web application, the listings database, admin role, sample apartment data, and the team's development skills (React/Vite). |
| **Key Activities** | Collect and verify listings, run search/filter, show details and status, manage the roommate board, and maintain the admin dashboard. |
| **Key Partners** | Apartment owners near campus, the university student council/housing office, student communities, and (later) local condos and agents who post directly. |
| **Cost Structure** | Hosting/domain, development and maintenance time, admin moderation effort, and (optionally) the Google Maps API. |

## Notes
This canvas is grounded in Lab 03 evidence (116 of 120 students used 3+ scattered sources; 71 of 120 also sought a roommate) and the Lab 04 requirements. The MVP prioritizes usage value (students finding rooms) over direct revenue.
