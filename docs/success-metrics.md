# Success Metrics and Decision Rules

## 1. Success Metrics
| Metric ID | Metric | Target | How to Measure | Related Requirement |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| M-01 | Task completion rate | 70% or higher | Completed tasks / total tasks | FR-02 |
| M-02 | Search success rate | 70% or higher | Users who find a suitable room / total users | FR-06 |
| M-03 | Filter usage success | 70% or higher | Users who filter by budget/distance correctly / total users | FR-06 |
| M-04 | Contact-owner completion | 70% or higher | Users who reach the owner-contact step / total users | FR-02 |
| M-05 | Time to find a room | Under 3 minutes | Average time from home page to a suitable listing | FR-06, FR-07 |
| M-06 | Usability rating | Average 4 out of 5 | Feedback form rating | FR-13 |
| M-07 | Map usefulness rating | Average 4 out of 5 | Feedback form rating | FR-07 |
| M-08 | Feature usefulness rating | Average 4 out of 5 | Feedback form rating | MVP features |

## 2. Decision Rules
| Result | Decision |
|---|---|
| Metrics meet target | Continue to the implementation sprint and keep the current design. |
| Some metrics fail | Revise the wireframe, user flow, labels, or feature priority before implementation. |
| Most metrics fail | Revisit the requirements, user stories, and Lab 03 problem evidence before building further. |
