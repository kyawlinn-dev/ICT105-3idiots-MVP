# Customer Discovery Questions

## Project Title

Student Apartment Finder

## Target Respondent

Students who searched for off-campus housing near campus in the last 6 months (especially first-year and international students), and a few apartment owners who rent to students.

## Consent and Context

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project?
2. Your participation is voluntary and anonymous. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience

3. Tell us about the last time you looked for an apartment near campus.
4. What were you trying to find, and what was your situation at that time?
5. What happened from start to finish — where did you begin and how did it end?

## Current Workaround

6. Where do you usually look for available rooms or apartments now?
7. What tools, apps, groups, or people do you currently use to find a place?
8. What is difficult, slow, confusing, or frustrating about the way you search now?

## Pain Severity and Consequence

9. How long did the search take you, and how often do you need to do this?
10. How serious is this problem for you on a scale of 1–5? Why?
11. What happens, or what did you lose, when the search takes too long or goes wrong?

## Roommate and Owner (case-specific)

12. Did you also look for a roommate to share the cost? How did you go about it?
13. (Owners) How do you currently advertise rooms to students, and what is hard about reaching them?

## Closing

14. Is there anything else we should understand about finding housing near campus?
15. May we use your anonymized response as part of our course project evidence?
